# Progress Log

Append-only log of what each iteration attempted and the outcome. The
operator reads this in the morning to understand what happened overnight.

Every iteration should append an entry in the format:

    [YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM] iter-NNN: <task-id> — <outcome>
    notes: <one line about what worked, what didn't, what blocked>

Different purpose from LEARNINGS.md:
  - LEARNINGS.md = durable knowledge, read by future iterations
  - progress.txt = operational history, read by the operator

Rules:
  - Append only. Never rewrite or delete existing entries.
  - One entry per iteration, even on failure.
  - Include the task ID so entries can be correlated with tasks.md.
